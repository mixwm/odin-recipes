Tommy's Margarita

Description

This is the world's most popular cocktail.

It is a simple recipe of only three ingredients and usually enjoyed on hot & sunny days.

Ingredients

  • 50ml Tequila
  • 25ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • 25ml Agave Syrup

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to a cocktail shaker
  2. Fill shaker with ice
  3. Shake until properly chilled and diluted
  4. Wipe lime around rim of tumbler and dip rim in salt
  5. Fill tumbler with ice
  6. Strain cocktail into tumbler
  7. Garnish drink with lime wedge
