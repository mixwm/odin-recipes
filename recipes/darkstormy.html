Dark & Stormy

Description

This is a classic rum drink that is very popular in the Caribbean islands.

It is a simple recipe of only four ingredients and usually enjoyed on days as a refreshing sipper.

Ingredients

  • 50ml Dark Rum
  • 15ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • 100ml Ginger Beer
  • 3 Dashes Angostura Bitters

Steps

  1. Pour Rum & Lime into a tall glass
  2. Fill glass with ice
  3. Add Ginger Beer
  4. Stir until properly mixed
  5. Add Bitters to the top of the drink
  6. Garnish drink with Lime wheels
