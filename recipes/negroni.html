Negroni

Description

This is one of the world's most popular cocktails and it's origin is shrouded in mystery.

It is a simple recipe of only three ingredients and usually enjoyed both before and after a meal.

Ingredients

  • 25ml Gin
  • 25ml Campari
  • 25ml Sweet Vermouth

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to a mixing glass
  2. Fill mixing glass with ice
  3. Stir until properly chilled and diluted
  4. Add large ice cube to tumbler
  5. Strain cocktail into tumbler over large cube
  6. Cut wide strip of peel from an orange
  7. Zest peel over drink to express oils
  8. Garnish drink with orange zest
